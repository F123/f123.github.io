  Let's imagine you need to prepare a simple outline for an upcoming written project. You can do this in emacs.

1. Open a document in emacs in text mode. You can do this by opening a file with the .text extension.

c-x c-f outline.txt

2. After opening the file, you want to initiate outline mode.

m-x outline-mode

3. Now let's write some first level headings.

* Prologue

Say a little bit about why you want to write this document.

* Introduction

Say something about your subject.

* The Meat of Your Project

** A million explanations

* Conclusion

* Epilogue
